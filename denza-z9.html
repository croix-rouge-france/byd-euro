Discover the BYD Denza Z9 GT

The BYD Denza Z9 GT represents the future of electric mobility. Combining exceptional performance, impressive range, and cutting-edge technologies, this electric car redefines the driving experience.

Its aerodynamic and futuristic design sacrifices nothing in comfort, offering a spacious and luxurious interior for all passengers.

  • 100% electric with zero emissions
  • Unmatched performance with acceleration from 0 to 100 km/h in 1.6 seconds
  • Futuristic design with Falcon Wing doors
